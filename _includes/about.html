In a world where there are more and more data publishers and data users, the research of those data is still a pain. Our aim with Hunter is to help publishers seeing their data reused and to help data users to find them quicker than ever and to handle them easily.

We believe that pre-processing data is a bad idea and that hosting them could create problems for data users. We just want to show you the way to the data you want as faster as possible!

We are two french Open Data enthusiast, exploring the new ways to consume data for a few years. We’ve blog about data for a certain time and now it’s time to action!

Hunter is, and will remain free - as in free beer - for its common usage.
